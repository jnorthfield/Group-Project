Hey guys! I’m really excited to tell you all about this new project I’ve been working on. I have started to make recordings of sounds you’d hear in your everyday life. I want to loop them and layer them to create a few tracks. I want to show that music can be accessible to everyone, that you don’t need expensive instruments or equipment to make good music. Remember, music is all around us, you just need to listen for it. I can’t wait to share the new tracks with you guys, subscribe at the bottom of the page to get an email when I post them!
