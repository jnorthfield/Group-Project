About Nathan

  Nathan James is an experimental solo artist from Rochester, New York. He has always had an interest in music, and started out his music career in a punk-rock band called Ghost Righter. He is also the owner and operator of Harmonious Recording Solutions where he helps others record and produce their music. Now, Nathan James is focusing on producing his own music in which he experiments with mixing many different genres including jazz, punk, classical, and rock. His most recent endeavor includes using everyday objects to create music. Nathan recognizes the importance of feedback and appreciates any comments (compliments or criticisms) that you provide on his blog.
